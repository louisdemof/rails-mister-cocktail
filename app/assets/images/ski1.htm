Product information

The Salomon Force 9 Ski is a great on-piste ski for someone looking for speed without any compromise to stability. Part of a new generation of wider bodied piste ski that can carve its way down anything, this ski received universal glowing reviews from our test team.

A new construction method for the tip now has lateral TPU inserts replacing previously used titanium. These act as bumpers and better absorb shock and chatter in mixed conditions, while a layer of carbon further improves dampening properties. This results in a hard-charging ski that can push through all terrain variations with ease, allowing you to put the pedal to the metal.

These skis come with a set of Z10 GW system bindings included. The bindings won't come mounted for safety reasons, but binding mounting can be performed free-of-charge at any Snow + Rock store. Just make sure to bring one of your ski boots with you!

Product features

  • Profile: Rocker Tip, Camber Under-Foot, Flat Tail
  • Core: Poplar Wood
  • Reinforcement: Single Layer Titanium, Rubber Insert
  • Top Sheet: TPU
  • Full Sandwich Sidewalls
  • Binding: System Mount

About Salomon About Salomon

Few names are as inextricably linked to the Alps as Salomon, founded in 1947 in Annecy, the heart of the Alps. It started life in François Salomon’s small workshop with the help of his wife and son.  Salomon have been reinventing gear, clothing and themselves for decades. This season look out for their Ice Star 3-layer jacket. It’s an all-mountain shell that’s extremely versatile whether you’re cruising groomers or chasing powder lines in waist deep snow.
